Banana Bread

Finally - banana bread, perfected!

Allrecipes Source

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease a 9x5 in loaf pan.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt into the bowl.
  3. Stir bananas, milkm and cinnaon into another bowl.
  4. Beat sugar and butter together in a third bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy; add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Stir banana mixture into butter mixture; mix in flour mixture until just blended. Fold in choclate chips; pour butter into prepared loaf pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean (about 70 min). Cool in pan for 10 min before removing to cool completely on a wire rack before slicing.
  7. Slice and enjoy!
