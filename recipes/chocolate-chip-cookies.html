Chocolate Chip Cookies

This award-winning chocolate chip cookie recipe is sure to blow you away!

Allrecipes Source

Ingredients

  • 4.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups butter, softened
  • 1.5 cups packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 (3.4oz) packages instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Sift together flour and baking soda in a medium bowl, set aside.
  3. Cream together butter, brown sugar, and white sugar in a large bowl. Beat in instant pudding mix until blended. Stir in eggs and vanilla.
  4. Add flour mixture to the butter mixture until it's well incoroprated. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop cookies by rounded spoonfuls onton ungreased cookie sheets.
  6. Bake in batches in the preheated oven until the edges are golden brown, 10-12 min, switching racks halfway through.
