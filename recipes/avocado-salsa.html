Avocado Salsa

This combination of avocados and hot peppers are sure to be a hit at your next party!

This is an original recipe by Steffan.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 4 serrano peppers
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup shallot
  • 1 cup cilantro
  • 1.5 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 lime (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Lightly roast the serrano peppers and garlic cloves.
  2. Cut up ingredients - including the avocado, serranos, garlic, shallot, and cilantro. Place all ingredients into food processor.
  3. Process the ingredients until a pulp is formed.
  4. Add salt, white wine vinegar, and water to mix. Continue processing until desired salsa consistency is formed. More water can be added if necessary.
  5. Squeeze in some lime to taste. Stir when added.
  6. Pour completed salsa into bowl and enjoy!
